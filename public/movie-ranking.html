Asur

Another Indian Web Series based on the RAW officer (Dhananjay Rajput). The Series is totally based on solving the case called the Asur(Devil).In this Web Series the Criminal develops a AI algorithm that predicts the behaviour and tells the next text steps of humans and start killing the people who are going to be dangerous or obstacles in his way.
Both the Season 1 and 2 are mind blowing describing how they reach the serial killer. But this is not the ends ....just waiting for season 3...
